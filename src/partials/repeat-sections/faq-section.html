Frequently Asked Questions

  • Virtual reality (VR) is a technology that uses headsets or other devices to create a simulated environment. Users can interact with this environment as if it were real, allowing for immersive experiences in gaming, education, training, and more.

  • We offer a range of virtual reality services including custom VR app development, immersive training simulations, VR tours, and interactive experiences for education, entertainment, and business. Whether you're looking to build a VR game or a training module, we tailor solutions to meet your goals.

  • Our pricing depends on the complexity, duration, and features of the VR project. Basic experiences start at a few thousand dollars, while more advanced simulations or applications can range higher. We provide custom quotes after a brief consultation to understand your needs and goals.

  • Most of our VR experiences are compatible with popular headsets like the Meta Quest, HTC Vive, and Valve Index. Some applications also work on mobile VR platforms or desktop with minimal requirements. We’ll guide you on the best setup based on your project.

  • Yes, we offer demos and prototypes for select VR experiences so you can explore the look and feel before moving forward. These previews help you understand the potential and scope of the solution we’re creating and ensure it aligns with your expectations.
